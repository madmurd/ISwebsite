Hi, I'm Maddie



My favorite quote is "the unexamined life is not worth living" by Socrates


This quote speaks to me because I believe a well-lived life is made up of a wide range of experiences that stretch us as people, teach us how to empathize with others, and create memories that shape who we are. I am an Experience Design major at BYU, so I dedicate a lot of my time towards learning how to create experiences for people that will help them live better, more intentional lives. Because of this, I am passionate about creating human connection, having FUN, and making the most out of life.



Fun Facts About Me


I love music (LANY and Taylor Swift are my favorites!), going to concerts, HIGH fit, playing card games (I'll challenge you in Skull King), hiking, skiing, reading, playing pickleball, eating ice cream (have you tried banana with Reese's cups?), dancing, going to Disneyland, and traveling.
